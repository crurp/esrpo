Our Story

Bred with Heart,
Raised with Purpose

Boundless Bullmastiffs is a small, dedicated kennel founded by Debbe, whose journey began with Jake — a magnificent Grand Champion who became a beacon of gentleness and strength. Jake's extraordinary compassion as a therapy companion for Debbe's father following a stroke revealed the true soul of this noble breed.

That experience inspired the acquisition of Shiloh, and together they produced a landmark first litter: eleven puppies, seven of whom achieved their AKC Championship by twenty months of age — three earning Grand Champion status.
