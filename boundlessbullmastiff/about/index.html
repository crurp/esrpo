Our Story

Bred with Heart,
Raised with Purpose

Boundless Bullmastiffs is a small, dedicated kennel founded by Debbe, whose journey began with Jake — a magnificent Grand Champion who became a beacon of gentleness and strength. Jake's extraordinary compassion as a therapy companion for Debbe's father following a stroke revealed the true soul of this noble breed.

That experience inspired the acquisition of Shiloh, and together they produced a landmark first litter: eleven puppies, seven of whom achieved their AKC Championship by twenty months of age — three earning Grand Champion status. The future is truly boundless.

Every puppy born at Boundless receives meticulous care, early neurological stimulation, and deep socialization to ensure they thrive in both show rings and loving homes.

The Breeder

Meet Debbe

Debbe's love for the Bullmastiff breed is deeply personal. It began not in a show ring, but in the quiet moments after her father's stroke — when Jake, her first Grand Champion, became an indispensable therapeutic companion, helping to restore communication and bring comfort to a difficult time.

That bond convinced Debbe that the Bullmastiff's greatest quality is not its conformation — it is its heart. Since then, she has built a breeding program that honors both: producing dogs that win in the ring and bring immeasurable joy at home.

Debbe's program is deliberately small and intentional. Every pairing is planned to advance health, structure, and temperament. Every puppy is treated as if it were her own — because in many ways, it always will be.

Our Philosophy

Sound in Body and Temperament

Every decision in the Boundless program flows from one principle: producing Bullmastiffs that are structurally correct, temperamentally excellent, and deeply loved.

Health First

All breeding stock carries full OFA health clearances — hips, elbows, heart, eyes, and thyroid. We pursue CHIC certification on every dog we breed from.

Breed Standard

We study pedigrees deeply and select for the traits that define the ideal Bullmastiff — headpiece, bone, movement, coat, and above all, that unmistakable gentle expression.

Temperament

The Bullmastiff must be fearless yet docile, alert yet calm. Every Boundless dog is evaluated for temperament and socialized extensively from the first days of life.

Lifetime Partnership

Placing a Boundless puppy is the beginning of a relationship, not the end. Every owner has a lifelong partner in Debbe — for questions, challenges, and celebrations.
